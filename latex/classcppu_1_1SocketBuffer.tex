Preserves record boundaries when exchanging data between connected TCP/\+IP sockets. This class ensures that one call to \mbox{\hyperlink{classcppu_1_1SocketBuffer_a92ae0351aaee8719d34e8c4618495d59}{write\+Line()}} corresponds to one and exactly one call to \mbox{\hyperlink{classcppu_1_1SocketBuffer_a222769d3776b9cbd3a727ee1f0e60358}{read\+Line()}} on the other side. This differs from the behavior of \mbox{\hyperlink{classcppu_1_1Socket_aeac77f859159715e2d63a5a0dc118788}{Socket\+::send()}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{classcppu_1_1Socket_a37c382af52cc02f92c0e19a0c6e0e04f}{Socket\+::receive()}} because TCP/\+IP connected sockets do not preserve record boundaries. \mbox{\hyperlink{classcppu_1_1SocketBuffer_a92ae0351aaee8719d34e8c4618495d59}{write\+Line()}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{classcppu_1_1SocketBuffer_a222769d3776b9cbd3a727ee1f0e60358}{read\+Line()}} solve this problem by automatically adding and searching for a separator between successive lines.
